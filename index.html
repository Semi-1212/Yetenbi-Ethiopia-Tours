A Day at Entoto Park

Escape the city's hustle and enjoy breathtaking views at Addis Ababa's beautiful highland park.

The park offers numerous hiking trails through lush vegetation, with panoramic views of Addis Ababa. At 3,000 meters above sea level, it's the perfect escape from the city heat.

  • Open daily from 6am to 6pm
  • Best time to visit: Morning hours

History June 2, 2023

Exploring the National Museum

Meet 'Lucy,' the world's most famous fossil at this must-visit cultural institution.

The National Museum of Ethiopia houses the 3.2 million-year-old remains of Lucy, along with other archaeological treasures. The museum is divided into four main sections:

  1. Paleontology section with fossil displays
  2. Prehistoric and ancient artifacts
  3. Ethnographic exhibits
  4. Modern art gallery

Guided tours are available in multiple languages.

Culture June 18, 2023

Shopping at Merkato

Navigate the largest open-air market in Africa with vibrant stalls.

Merkato is spread over several square kilometers with distinct sections:

  • Spice market with aromatic Ethiopian spices
  • Textile and clothing section
  • Traditional crafts and souvenirs
  • Fresh produce and coffee beans

Tips for visitors: Arrive early, bring small bills, and be prepared to bargain.

Nightlife July 5, 2023

Addis Ababa After Dark

Experience the vibrant nightlife from jazz clubs to traditional music venues.

Addis comes alive at night with diverse entertainment options:

  • Jazz clubs: Jazzamba Lounge, Mama's Kitchen
  • Traditional music: Habesha Cultural Restaurant
  • Modern clubs: Illusion, Gaslight
  • Rooftop bars: Skyline Hotel, The Roof

Most venues are open until 2am on weekends.

Food July 12, 2023

A Taste of Ethiopia

Discover the rich flavors of traditional Ethiopian cuisine in Addis' best restaurants.

Must-try dishes in Addis Ababa:

  • Injera: The spongy sourdough flatbread
  • Doro Wat: Spicy chicken stew
  • Kitfo: Minced raw beef delicacy
  • Tibs: Sauteed meat dishes

Top restaurants include Yod Abyssinia, 2000 Habesha, and Totot Traditional Restaurant.
